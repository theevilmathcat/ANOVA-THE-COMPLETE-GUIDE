üîç Introduction

ANOVA (Analysis of Variance) is a statistical method for comparing group means by analyzing variances. This guide explains:

    How ANOVA partitions total variability into between-group and within-group components

    The F-ratio calculation and interpretation

    Why we analyze variances to compare means

    Practical applications in weightlifting research

2. By Design

    Mixed models with both fixed and random effects

    Greenhouse-Geisser/Huynh-Feldt corrections for repeated measures
